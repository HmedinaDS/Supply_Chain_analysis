# Supply_Chain_analysis
📌 Strategic Takeaways from the Supply Chain Analysis
Predictive Operational Efficiency: 
By leveraging advanced machine learning, specifically through our XGBoost model, the organization can now accurately predict supply chain bottlenecks, defect rates, and delivery performance before they impact the bottom line. This shifts our operations from a reactive firefighting approach to a proactive risk-mitigation strategy.

Defect Reduction & Quality Control: 
The data analysis exposed critical variances in defect rates across different product lines. With these insights, management can pinpoint which manufacturing stages or suppliers require immediate quality audits, directly reducing waste, lowering return rates, and protecting brand equity.

Resource & Logistics Optimization: 
While simpler models like Logistic Regression provided basic visibility, the final ensemble models successfully captured the non-linear complexities of our logistics data. This allows the business to optimize inventory levels, streamline shipping routes, and improve supplier lead times, translating into significant cost savings and a higher Service Level Agreement (SLA) fulfillment rate.

Final Business Recommendation:
Implementing the XGBoost pipeline into production will enable data-driven scheduling and automated alerts for high-risk shipments. This integration is projected to optimize supply chain visibility, reduce operational overhead, and significantly improve overall customer satisfaction.
